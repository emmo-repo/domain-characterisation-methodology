References
==========

CHAMEO has been developed by `Goldbeck Consulting LTD <https://materialsmodelling.com/>`__ within the `NanoMECommons <https://www.nanomecommons.net/>`__ project and is supported by the `European Materials Modelling Council  <https://emmc.eu/>`__.

CHAMEO is closely aligned with two major standardization efforts:

1. **Elementary Multiperspective Material Ontology (EMMO)**: CHAMEO is a domain ontology developed under the `EMMO framework <https://w3id.org/emmo/>`__. This ensures that it shares a common foundational structure with other materials-related ontologies, facilitating seamless integration within the broader EMMO ecosystem.

2. **Characterization Data (CHADA) Framework**: The initial development of CHAMEO is based on the CHADA documentation scheme introduced in the CEN Workshop Agreement (`CWA 17815 <https://www.cencenelec.eu/media/CEN-CENELEC/CWAs/ICT/cwa17815.pdf>`__). CHADA provides guidelines for structuring characterization data, which CHAMEO translates into an ontology-driven framework. This work on CHAMEO has been used as the basis for defining the new CHADA template, leading to the release of `CWA 17815:2025 <https://www.cencenelec.eu/media/CEN-CENELEC/CWAs/RI/2025/cwa17815_2025.pdf>`__.
