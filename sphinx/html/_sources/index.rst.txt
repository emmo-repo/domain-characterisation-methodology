=========================
CHAMEO Ontology
=========================

Welcome to the **CHAracterisation MEthodology Ontology (CHAMEO)**, a semantic framework designed to harmonize materials characterisation methodologies. CHAMEO provides a standardized terminology and structure to model common aspects across various characterisation techniques, facilitating interoperability and knowledge sharing in materials science.
